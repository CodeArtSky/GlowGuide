DESCRIPTION
================================================================================
Get personalized makeup looks powered by AI! GlowGuide creates custom beauty recommendations based on your unique features, the occasion, and how you want to feel.

1. Tell us about yourself - skin tone, hair color, eye color, and preferences
2. Select your occasion - date night, work, casual, party, and more
3. Choose your mood - confident, natural, glamorous, edgy, and more
4. Get instant AI-generated beauty recommendations tailored just for you

FEATURES:
- AI-Powered Recommendations: Our smart AI analyzes your features and preferences to create personalized looks
- 8 Occasions: Date Night, Work/Office, Casual Day, Party/Night Out, Wedding/Formal, Workout, Beach/Pool, Special Event
- 8 Moods: Confident, Natural, Glamorous, Edgy, Romantic, Fresh, Bold, Sophisticated
- Save Favorites: Keep your favorite looks for easy reference
- Share Looks: Create beautiful social media-ready graphics of your looks
