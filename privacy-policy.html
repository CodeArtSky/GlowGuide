GlowGuide Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to GlowGuide ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Onboarding Preferences: Skin tone, hair color, eye color, and beauty preferences you select during setup
  • Generated Looks: Beauty recommendations you save to your favorites
  • Usage Data: Number of looks generated and saved (for free tier tracking)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status managed through Apple's App Store

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate personalized makeup and beauty recommendations
  • Save your favorite looks for future reference
  • Track usage for free tier limitations
  • Process and manage your subscription through Apple
  • Improve our app and services

4. AI-Generated Content

GlowGuide uses artificial intelligence to generate beauty recommendations. Your preferences are processed by AI services to create personalized content. We do not store your preferences on external servers beyond what is necessary for generating recommendations.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. We do not maintain external databases containing your personal information. Subscription information is managed entirely through Apple's App Store infrastructure.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For managing in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • OpenAI API: For generating AI-powered beauty recommendations

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

GlowGuide is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored within the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app or using the "Reset Onboarding" feature
  • Cancel your subscription through your Apple ID settings

10. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Data stored on your device is protected by iOS security features.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
